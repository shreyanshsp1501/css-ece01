A laptop is a portable personal computer with a clamshell design that includes a screen, keyboard, and a pointing device like a touchpad, all integrated into a single, lightweight device. It is powered by a battery, allowing users to work, study, or entertain themselves anywhere, from a coffee shop to an airplane, making it essential for modern mobility. Laptops can connect to the internet, run various software, and often include a webcam, microphone, and speakers, providing the same functionality as a desktop computer in a more compact form factor
